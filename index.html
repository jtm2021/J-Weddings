About Us

Frequently Asked Questions

J Weddings offers a range of wedding planning services, including full-service wedding planning, partial planning, and day-of coordination. We also offer vendor referrals and management, event design, decor, and more.

Full-service wedding planning is a comprehensive planning package that covers all aspects of your wedding. Our team will work with you from the beginning stages of planning to the final touches on your wedding day to ensure a seamless and stress-free experience.

Partial planning is a more customized planning package that allows you to choose which services you need assistance with. This package is ideal for couples who have already started planning but need help with specific aspects of their wedding.

Day-of coordination is a service that allows you to sit back and relax on your wedding day while our team ensures everything runs smoothly. We will oversee the logistics, vendors, and timeline to make sure your day is stress-free.

Yes, we offer destination wedding planning services to help you plan your dream wedding in any location. We can assist with travel arrangements, accommodations, vendor selection, and much more.

Our pricing varies depending on the services you need and the size and complexity of your wedding. We offer a complimentary consultation to discuss your needs and provide a personalized quote.
